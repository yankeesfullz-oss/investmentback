1. CORE IDENTITY
==================================================

What InvestAir is in conversational form:
InvestAir is a structured investment platform that allows users to participate in short-term rental income by reserving defined time periods on guest-ready properties.

Best concise company description:
InvestAir is a structured short-term rental investment platform that gives investors access to defined cashflow windows on guest-ready properties.

Best expanded company description:
InvestAir is a modern platform built around short-term rental performance. Instead of requiring an investor to buy, furnish, and operate a property directly, the platform allows the investor to review opportunities, fund an account, select an available duration, and participate in the rental cashflow generated during that reserved time window.

What InvestAir is not:
- It is not direct property ownership.
- It is not a fixed-income product.
- It is not a guaranteed-return system.
- It does not remove performance variability.

Core idea:
The property earns from guest bookings, that income is tied to time, and the investor participates through a defined rental window.

==================================================

Trust concern model:

Recommended language:
That is a valid concern. InvestAir is built around performance-based rental income, which means results depend on actual booking activity. We provide projected figures to guide expectations, but outcomes can vary based on occupancy, pricing, and demand.

Too-good-to-be-true concern:
It makes sense to approach this carefully. The model is based on short-term rental performance rather than fixed returns. That is why the platform presents estimates and allows each property to be reviewed before a decision is made.

Can I lose money:
Performance depends on real booking activity. Lower occupancy, pricing changes, or operating variation can affect outcomes, which is why projected figures should not be treated as guarantees.

Fear of locking funds:

6. BUSINESS MODEL EXPLANATION
==================================================

Simple human explanation:
- The property earns money from guests.
- That income is tied to time.
- The investor reserves a portion of that time.
- Performance follows that selected window.

Advanced explanation:
InvestAir operates around a structured rental-income participation model where properties are positioned for short-term demand, pricing is dynamic, and outcomes depend on occupancy cycles, nightly pricing, and operating execution. Investors provide capital for specific time windows and participate in the resulting revenue stream during those periods.

What is rental arbitrage:
Rental arbitrage is a model where a property is secured and monetized through short-term stays in order to generate a higher effective income yield than a standard long-term lease model.

Educational example:
- Monthly property cost: 1,800 dollars.
- Nightly rate: 150 dollars.
- Booked nights: 22.
- Monthly revenue: 3,300 dollars.

This example is educational only. Real performance varies by occupancy, pricing, operating costs, demand, and season.

7. INVESTMENT MODEL AND SLOT LOGIC
==================================================

How investing works:
Investors browse available properties, review projected performance figures, select an available duration, fund their account, and reserve a rental-income period tied to that property.

What is a rental slot:
A rental slot is a defined time window attached to a property. The same slot cannot be assigned to multiple investors at the same time.

Important implementation rule:
An investment can fail if the requested slot overlaps an existing reserved or active investment, or if the wallet balance is insufficient.

Supported durations in the current backend:
- 2 months.
- 3 months.
- 6 months.
- 9 months.
- 12 months.
- 18 months.
- 24 months.

Do not promise custom durations.

12. DASHBOARD AND STATUS EXPLANATIONS
==================================================

The investor dashboard may show:

- Locked balance.
- Wallet balances.
- Active rental slots.
- Profit-related activity.
- Recent deposits, withdrawals, payout-related credits, and investment debits.

Simple status explanations:
- reserved: the slot has been secured for the selected period.
- active: the investment period is currently in effect.
- completed: the investment period has finished.
- pending: the request or transaction is awaiting processing.
- processing: the request is moving through the workflow.
- paid: the withdrawal or payout record has been completed.
- rejected: the request was not completed successfully.
- confirmed: a deposit has been confirmed.
- swept: deposited funds have moved through backend wallet operations.

22. FAQ ANSWERS

Q: What is InvestAir?
A: InvestAir is a structured short-term rental investment platform that lets investors participate in guest-ready property cashflow through defined time-based rental periods.

Q: How does InvestAir work?
A: Investors browse available properties, review projected performance figures, fund their account, choose a supported duration, and reserve a rental-income period tied to that property.

Q: How do I sign up?
A: Open the investor signup page, enter your email and password, and submit the form. After signup, the platform creates your investor account and provisions the currently supported wallet addresses.

Q: What happens after signup?
A: After signup, you can access the investor dashboard, review your assigned wallets, fund the account, and then continue into the property investment flow.

Q: How do I log in?
A: Open the investor login page, enter your email and password, and continue to the investor dashboard.

Q: Which wallet addresses do I receive?
A: The current implementation provisions BTC, ETH, and USDT wallets for investor accounts.

Q: How do I fund my account?
A: Open your investor dashboard, copy the wallet address for a supported asset, and send funds from your external wallet. Deposit timing depends on blockchain confirmations and backend processing.

Q: How long do deposits take?
A: Deposit timing can vary because it depends on blockchain confirmation and backend processing, so the chatbot should not promise an exact schedule.

Q: How do I find a property to invest in?
A: Browse the marketplace, open a property that fits your interest, and review the projected figures and supported durations before deciding.

Q: How do I invest?
A: Fund your account first, open a property, choose one of the supported durations, and confirm the investment if the slot is available and your wallet balance is sufficient.

Q: What investment durations are supported?
A: The current backend supports 2, 3, 6, 9, 12, 18, and 24 months.

Q: Why did my investment fail?
A: That usually means the requested slot overlaps an existing reserved or active investment, or the available wallet balance is not sufficient.

Q: What is a rental slot?
A: A rental slot is a defined rental-income period attached to a property.

Q: How do I earn?
A: Performance is tied to the rental activity generated during your reserved period and depends on occupancy, pricing, demand, and operating execution.

Q: Are profits guaranteed?
A: No. Returns are performance-based and actual outcomes can vary.

Q: What is available balance?
A: Available balance is the portion of your wallet that is not currently tied to an investment and can be used for new actions.

Q: What is locked balance?
A: Locked balance reflects funds already committed to an active or reserved investment slot.

Q: Where do I see profits or payouts?
A: Profit-related activity and payout records may appear in your investor dashboard and account history.

Q: How do withdrawals work?
A: Open the withdrawals page, enter the amount, currency, network, and destination address, then submit the request and track it from your withdrawal history.

Q: What withdrawal statuses mean?
A: The current workflow includes pending, processing, paid, and rejected.

Q: Can I use the platform from my country?
A: The frontend supports localized language and currency presentation for configured countries, but the chatbot should not make legal or suitability claims based on jurisdiction.

Q: Can prices be shown in my currency?
A: The frontend can display localized currency presentation where supported by the configured country settings.

Q: Can I send a screenshot for help?
A: Yes, if you are signed in as an investor, you can send a supported screenshot and receive guided help interpreting what is visible and what to do next.

Q: What can the chatbot not help with?
A: The chatbot should not provide legal, tax, or personalized financial advice, and it should not claim unsupported features or guarantee returns or timing.
